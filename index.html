Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. The arrangement of type involves selecting typefaces, point sizes, line lengths, line-spacing, and letter-spacing, and adjusting the space between pairs of letters. Typography has evolved dramatically over centuries, from hand-carved woodblocks to movable type to digital fonts that we use on screens today.
